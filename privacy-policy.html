Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

JEE Formula Sheets - RealSem ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by RealSem EdTech. This privacy policy describes how we handle user data.

Data We Collect

The app collects minimal, anonymous usage data to improve the user experience:

  • Analytics events (via Mixpanel): app opens, chapters viewed, formulas viewed, searches performed, bookmarks added/removed, and theme changes. These events are anonymous and not linked to any personal identity.
  • Advertising data (via Google AdMob): AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal information.
  • We do not require user accounts or login.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not share or sell any user data to third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device

Bookmarks and theme preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Mixpanel - anonymous analytics. Mixpanel Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob - advertising. Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
